# Identity Verification Requirements

## Required Identity Documents

Every new customer must provide one government-issued photo ID (passport,
driver's license, or national ID card) and one proof of address dated
within the last 90 days (utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement).
Document images must be legible and unexpired at the time of submission.

## Address Verification

The address on the proof-of-address document must match the address on
the account application. Mismatches must be resolved before the account
moves out of pending status; unresolved mismatches after 30 days require
the case to be sent to manual review.

## Minors and Restricted Accounts

Applicants under 18 may not open an account independently and require a
verified adult co-signer. Accounts flagged as belonging to a restricted
jurisdiction on the sanctions list must not be verified through the
standard flow and must be routed to Enhanced Due Diligence.
