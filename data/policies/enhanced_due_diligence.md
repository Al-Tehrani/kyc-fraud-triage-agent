# Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)

## When EDD Applies

Enhanced Due Diligence is required for: customers from a high-risk
jurisdiction, PEP matches, accounts requesting transaction limits above
the standard tier, and any case where standard identity verification
could not be completed.

## EDD Requirements

EDD adds a source-of-funds declaration, a second independent identity
document, and a supervisor sign-off before the case can be approved. EDD
cases may not be auto-approved by the agent under any fraud score band.

## Ongoing Monitoring

Accounts that underwent EDD are subject to quarterly re-screening against
the sanctions list and a review of transaction patterns for the first 12
months after approval.
