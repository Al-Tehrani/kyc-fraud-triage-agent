# Sanctions and PEP Screening Policy

## Screening Triggers

Every applicant's full legal name and date of birth must be screened
against the sanctions and politically exposed persons (PEP) watchlist at
account opening and again on any material change to identity information.

## Match Handling

A confirmed name-and-date-of-birth match on the sanctions list results in
mandatory escalation; the account may not be approved or left in review
status. A PEP match without a sanctions hit does not require escalation
on its own but must trigger Enhanced Due Diligence.

## False Positive Review

Partial name matches with a differing date of birth are treated as
candidate false positives. These may be cleared by a compliance analyst
who documents the reason for dismissal; they must not be auto-cleared by
the agent.
